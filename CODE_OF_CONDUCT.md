# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, race, gender, disability, ethnicity, religion, or level of experience.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Showing empathy toward others

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory jokes
- Trolling or public/private harassment
- Publishing private information without consent
