Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is SmartTalk?

SmartTalk is a real-time messaging app that lets users chat, share files, and connect instantly with friends or groups.

2. Is SmartTalk free to use?

Yes! SmartTalk is completely free for all users with no hidden charges.

3. How do I create an account?

Just click on the Register button, fill in your details, and start chatting.

4. Can I edit my profile information?

Yes, you can go to the Profile section and click Edit Profile to update your details.

5. Is SmartTalk available on mobile devices?

For now, SmartTalk is web-based, but mobile support is coming soon.

6. How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click Forgot Password, and follow the steps to reset it.

7. Can I send private messages?

Yes, private messaging is supported. Click a user and start chatting privately.

8. Are my messages encrypted?

Yes. We use secure protocols to ensure your messages are private and safe.

9. Can I share images or files in chat?

Absolutely. SmartTalk supports file sharing (images, docs, etc.).

10. Is there an admin dashboard?

Yes. Admins can access special tools through the Admin Panel.

11. Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can request account deletion by contacting us at smarttalkgit@gmail.com.

12. How do I know who is online?

Online users are listed in real-time on your dashboard or chat page.

13. Does SmartTalk support notifications?

Yes. You'll receive alerts for new messages and updates.

14. What should I do if I find a bug or issue?

Please report bugs or issues by emailing smarttalkgit@gmail.com.

15. Can I export my chat history?

Yes. You can export your chat history from the settings or message page.
